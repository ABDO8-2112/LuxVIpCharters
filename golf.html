Luxury Transport for Golf Enthusiasts

Western Australia boasts some of the most beautiful golf courses in the country. Experience them in style with Lux VIP Charters. We provide spacious, comfortable transport for you and your golfing partners, with plenty of room for your clubs and equipment.

Whether you're heading to Joondalup Resort, The Vines, or Lake Karrinyup, our chauffeurs will ensure you arrive relaxed and ready to play. Enjoy a hassle-free day on the green with our dedicated golf transfer service.

Golf Tour Features:

  • Ample Storage: Our V-Class vehicles easily accommodate golf bags and luggage.
  • Group Transfers: Travel together with your playing partners.
  • Flexible Schedule: We work around your tee times.
  • Refreshments: Complimentary water provided for your journey.
