About Us

Excellence in Luxury Transport

Our Story

  • Lux VIP Charters Perth was created with a clear purpose: to redefine what luxury transport should feel like. More than simply moving clients from one place to another, we focus on delivering a journey marked by comfort, elegance, and unwavering reliability.
  • With years of experience in the chauffeur industry, we have become a trusted name for discerning travellers across Western Australia. Our pristine Mercedes-Benz fleet, paired with highly professional chauffeurs, allows us to offer a seamless experience across all services — from airport transfers and corporate travel to private events and special occasions.
  • At Lux VIP, we pride ourselves on exceptional service, meticulous attention to detail, and a commitment to customer satisfaction at every step. Whether you're travelling for business or celebration, your journey with us is crafted to be calm, comfortable, and effortlessly luxurious.

Meet the Team

Ken

  • Ken is the proud founder of Lux VIP Charters Perth, a premier chauffeur service dedicated to providing exceptional transportation experiences in Western Australia. With five years of hands-on experience, he ensures that every ride is not just a journey, but a memorable experience.
  • Growing up in a family deeply rooted in the transportation sector, Ken learned from his father, a seasoned professional with over 30 years of experience. Under his mentorship, Ken honed his skills in customer service, attention to detail, and building lasting client relationships — the legacy of excellence behind Lux VIP today.
  • Ken’s commitment is reflected in a meticulously maintained fleet, professional chauffeurs, and personalized service for corporate events, special occasions, and every journey in between. His hands-on approach and passion for luxury transport ensure each client receives comfort, safety, and a touch of elegance.
  • Looking ahead, Ken remains committed to the values instilled by his father, establishing Lux VIP Charters Perth as a leader in the industry. With a focus on reliability, professionalism, and exceptional service, Ken and his team elevate every journey to new heights.
