Booking Policies and Service Agreements

Booking Policy

All bookings are subject to availability and confirmation by Lux VIP Charters Perth. A deposit may be required to secure your reservation, especially for weddings and special events.

Cancellations

Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled pickup time may incur a cancellation fee. Cancellations made within 4 hours of the pickup time will be charged the full fare.

Waiting Time

Complimentary waiting time is included for airport pickups (up to 45 minutes after landing). For other pickups, a waiting time fee may apply after 15 minutes.

Passenger Conduct

Passengers are expected to behave in a respectful manner. Any damage caused to the vehicle by passengers will be charged to the booking client. Smoking and consumption of food are generally not permitted in the vehicles unless prior arrangements have been made.

Liability

Lux VIP Charters Perth is not liable for delays caused by traffic conditions, weather, or other unforeseen circumstances. However, we will always do our best to find alternative routes and ensure you reach your destination on time.
